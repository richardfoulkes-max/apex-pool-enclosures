APE-HC1 v2.0 - Acoustic Enclosure with Chimney
December 2025 | Apex Enclosures | Rev 2.0
REVISION 2.0 - MAJOR DESIGN CHANGE
This RFQ supersedes all previous versions. The enclosure now includes an acoustic chimney for top-exhaust heat pumps. This is a larger and more complex product than v1.0.

1. Company Introduction

Apex Enclosures is developing acoustic enclosures for residential pool heat pumps in the UAE and Gulf region. Pool heaters/chillers generate 55-70 dB(A) noise - a significant issue for villa environments. We are seeking manufacturing partners for prototype development and production.

Related Product Line: We are also developing outdoor TV enclosures (ATE series) and may combine orders for manufacturing efficiency. Same materials, same quality standards.

2. Product Overview

APE-HC1 v2.0: Acoustic Enclosure with Chimney

Reduces pool heat pump noise by 15-20 dB(A) while maintaining required airflow for equipment cooling. Features an acoustic chimney for top-exhaust heat pumps.

Base Enclosure:
1400 × 900 × 900mm (L × W × H)
Acoustic Chimney:
1400 × 900 × 600mm (L × W × H)
Total Height:
1500mm (base + chimney)
Target Noise Reduction:
15-20 dB(A)
Target Unit:
ROTEAM Europa 44kW heat pump
Weatherproofing:
IP65 (outdoor, Gulf climate)

2.1 Target Heat Pump Specifications

The enclosure is designed for this specific unit:

ParameterValue
ModelROTEAM Europa 00500450
Heating Capacity44 kW (150,000 BTU)
Cooling Capacity34 kW
Power380V / 3 Phase / 50Hz
Fan Configuration2 × 400mm TOP EXHAUST
Estimated Unit Size~1000 × 600 × 800mm
Critical Design Requirement: The heat pump has TOP-mounted exhaust fans (vertical airflow), NOT side exhaust. The enclosure requires an acoustic chimney to attenuate the upward exhaust noise. Standard side-to-side airflow enclosures will NOT work.

8. Assembly Features

8.1 Access Door

  • Full-height hinged door on front panel (below louvres)
  • Same acoustic construction as walls (double-skin with infill)
  • Continuous EPDM seal around perimeter
  • Lockable compression latches

8.2 Chimney Mounting

  • Chimney bolts to base enclosure via flange plate
  • Weatherproof EPDM gasket between chimney and base
  • Must be removable for installation (drop over equipment first, then mount chimney)

8.3 Base

  • Floor panel with rubber isolator feet
  • Equipment sits on floor panel
  • Bottom edge sealed with weatherstrip to concrete pad

10. Quality Requirements

  • All welds ground smooth, no sharp edges
  • Powder coat uniform, no runs, chips, or thin spots
  • Door gaskets continuous, proper compression when closed
  • Acoustic panels properly seated, no rattles or vibration
  • Louvre blades aligned, consistent spacing
  • Chimney-to-base seal weatherproof
  • Dimensional tolerance: ±3mm
