Request for Quotation

Acoustic Pool Equipment Enclosures - Prototype & Production
December 2025 | Apex Enclosures

1. Company Introduction

Apex Enclosures is developing a range of acoustic enclosures for residential pool equipment in the UAE and Gulf region. We are seeking manufacturing partners for prototype development and eventual production.

Related Product Line: We are also developing outdoor TV enclosures (ATE series) and may combine orders for manufacturing efficiency.

5. Quality Requirements

  • All welds ground smooth, no sharp edges
  • Powder coat uniform, no runs, chips, or thin spots
  • Gaskets continuous, no gaps at corners
  • Hardware operation smooth (hinges, latches)
  • Dimensional tolerance: ±3mm
  • APE-HC1: Acoustic panels properly seated, no rattles
