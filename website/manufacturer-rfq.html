Request for Quotation

APE-HC1 Acoustic Pool Heater/Chiller Enclosure
December 2025 | Apex Enclosures

1. Company Introduction

Apex Enclosures is developing acoustic enclosures for residential pool heat pumps in the UAE and Gulf region. Pool heaters/chillers generate 55-70 dB(A) noise - a significant issue for villa environments. We are seeking manufacturing partners for prototype development and production.

Related Product Line: We are also developing outdoor TV enclosures (ATE series) and may combine orders for manufacturing efficiency. Same materials, same quality standards.

2. Product Overview

APE-HC1: Acoustic Heater/Chiller Enclosure

Reduces pool heat pump noise by 15-20 dB(A) while maintaining required airflow for equipment cooling.

External Dimensions:
1200 × 700 × 900mm (W × D × H)
Target Noise Reduction:
15-20 dB(A)
Installation:
Above ground, on concrete pad
Weatherproofing:
IP65 (outdoor, Gulf climate)
Critical Design Challenge: This enclosure must BOTH reduce noise AND allow sufficient airflow. Heat pumps require ventilation - a sealed box would cause overheating. The acoustic louvres are the key component.

4. Acoustic Louvre Specifications (CRITICAL)

Key Component: The acoustic louvres are the most important part of this design. They must block sound while allowing airflow. Standard louvres will NOT work.
SpecificationRequirement
Louvre TypeZ-blade acoustic louvres (or chevron/bent blade)
Blade Angle45°
Blade Pitch50mm centre-to-centre
Blade Depth75mm minimum
Blade MaterialAluminum with acoustic infill (mineral wool or foam)
Free AreaMinimum 40% (equipment needs airflow)
Insertion LossTarget 10-15 dB

6. Assembly & Access

6.1 Door

  • Full-height hinged door on front panel
  • Same acoustic construction as walls (double-skin with infill)
  • Continuous EPDM seal around perimeter
  • Lockable compression latches

6.2 Base

  • Open bottom (equipment sits on concrete pad)
  • Enclosure drops over equipment
  • Bottom edge sealed with weatherstrip to pad
