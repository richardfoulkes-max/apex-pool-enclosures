Pricing Notes:
  • This is a significantly larger and more complex enclosure than v1.0 (~$1,450)
  • The acoustic chimney adds ~$470 in materials alone
  • Premium pricing justified by noise reduction value proposition
  • Volume production (5+ units) could reduce costs by 15-20%
  • Acoustic louvres are the highest-cost component - explore local fabrication
Cost Reduction Opportunities:
  • Louvres: Fabricate Z-blades locally instead of buying assemblies (-$200)
  • Chimney: Simplify to 3-sided lining if rear faces wall (-$40)
  • Volume: 5-10 unit batch reduces material cost 15% (-$300)
  • Potential optimized cost: ~$2,100 per unit
