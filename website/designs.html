CRITICAL RISK: Acoustic louvres reduce free area to 35%. Heat pump must have sufficient fan pressure to overcome this restriction. Verify equipment CFM and static pressure ratings before final design.
Noise Validation: 52.5mm acoustic panels (NRC 0.85) + Z-blade louvres (15-18 dB insertion loss) = expected total reduction of 15-18 dB. A 70 dB heat pump would measure 52-55 dB at 1m from enclosure exterior.

Thermal Margin: At 55°C ambient + 350W load + 120 CFM cooling, internal delta-T is approximately 10-15°C above ambient. Internal temperature: 65-70°C air, but TV surface receives direct airflow keeping display under 50°C. Marginal but acceptable. Recommend white powder coat to reduce solar absorption.
