APE-P Series - Pump Enclosure

Semi-Sunken Weatherproof Housing for Pool Pumps & Filtration
Model: APE-P1 | Rev 1.0 | December 2025

1. Product Overview

The APE-P series is a semi-sunken enclosure designed to house pool pumps, filters, and related equipment. The design integrates with villa landscaping while providing weatherproof protection and easy maintenance access.

Key Features

  • + Semi-sunken installation (300mm above grade)
  • + IP65 weatherproof rating
  • + Hinged lid with gas strut assist
  • + Integrated floor drainage
  • + Cable/pipe entry glands
  • + Padlock-ready security

Target Equipment

  • + Variable speed pool pumps (up to 3HP)
  • + Sand/cartridge filters
  • + Salt chlorinators
  • + Automation controllers
  • + Valves and plumbing manifolds

Installation Context

  • + Villa pool equipment areas
  • + Landscaped garden settings
  • + Adjacent to pool plant room
  • + Gulf climate (high UV, dust, humidity)

6. Installation Requirements

6.1 Site Preparation

  • Excavate pit to depth: enclosure height minus 300mm above-grade allowance
  • Level compacted base with 50mm sand/gravel bed
  • Install 100mm PVC drainage pipe to soak-away or storm drain
  • Route electrical conduit and plumbing before enclosure placement

6.2 Enclosure Installation

  • Lower enclosure into prepared pit
  • Level using adjustable feet or shims
  • Backfill around enclosure with compacted material
  • Connect drainage outlet to site drainage
  • Install cable glands and pipe grommets
  • Apply silicone sealant to all penetrations
