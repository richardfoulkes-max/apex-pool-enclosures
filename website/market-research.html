Key Insight: The Ventilation Paradox

Forum users consistently struggle with the same problem: heat pumps need airflow but enclosures block sound. Many DIY solutions fail because:

  • Standard enclosures trap heat and reduce efficiency
  • Opening vents for airflow lets sound escape
  • One user spent >£4,000 on an enclosure that "had no effect" because it didn't address low-frequency hum

Our solution: Acoustic louvres with Z-blade design that allow airflow while blocking sound.

4. UAE/Gulf Market Gap

Key Finding: No Dedicated Products in UAE

Our research found no purpose-built pool heater/chiller acoustic enclosures available in the UAE market. Current solutions are:

  • Custom HVAC soundproofing (expensive, long lead time)
  • Generic acoustic barriers (not designed for ventilation)
  • DIY solutions (inconsistent results)
  • Importing from Europe (high shipping costs, no local support)

UAE Market Characteristics

Factor Implication
High villa density Many pools with heat pumps in close proximity to neighbours
Outdoor living culture Evening pool use when noise is more noticeable
Premium expectations Willingness to pay for quality solutions
Harsh climate Products must be UV stable, weatherproof, heat resistant
Local manufacturing Fabrication capabilities exist (aluminium, powder coating)

5. Target Customer Profile

Primary: Villa Owners

  • Own pools with heat pump/chiller systems
  • Bothered by equipment noise during evening use
  • Value aesthetics (visible equipment area)
  • Budget: AED 5,000-10,000 for quality solution

Secondary: Pool Contractors

  • Install and service pool equipment
  • Receive noise complaints from clients
  • Need reliable product to recommend/install
  • Opportunity for trade pricing / partnerships
